HUGO BOSS

Bekijk

A German fashion brand, also known as simply Boss, Hugo Boss was founded in Metzingen, Germany, in 1924. The company was named after Hugo Boss, the brand’s creator who was born and died living his entire life in Metzingen. The company’s headquarters are still based in the town in which Hugo lived.
